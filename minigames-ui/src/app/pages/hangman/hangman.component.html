Hangman Game

How to Play

Test your vocabulary and guessing skills by uncovering the hidden word one letter at a time. Guess carefully, because each incorrect letter costs you a life.

  • Guess one letter at a time.
  • Incorrect guesses cost one life.
  • Reveal the word before all lives are lost.
  • Continue until you either reveal the entire word or run out of attempts.
Scoring
  • Win: Earn 1 point.
  • Loss: Earn 0 points.
  • Try to solve the word before running out of attempts to maximize your success rate.
