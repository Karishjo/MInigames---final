Formula Game

How to Play

Put your mathematical skills to the test by creating an expression that gets as close as possible to the target number.

  • Use the numbers provided at the start of the game.
  • Combine them using addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (*), and division (/).
  • You may use brackets to help build your calculation.
  • Each number can only be used once.
  • Your goal is to reach the target value or get as close to it as possible.

Scoring

  • Exact Match: Perfect score.
  • Within 10 of the target: Great job, you're very close.
  • More than 10 away: Nice try, keep practicing.
